01 / Working together

Fit, scope, and support

Start here if you are comparing writers, strategists, or project formats.

What is SEO content writing and how does it differ from regular content writing?
SEO content writing connects useful, persuasive writing with search intent, keyword evidence, on-page structure, and internal links. Regular content can communicate well; SEO content also helps the right audience discover it through search.
What industries or niches do you specialize in?
I have direct experience with insurance, licensing, government, legal, healthcare support, local business, and research-heavy service content. I can support other industries when reliable subject-matter sources and a clear review process are available.
Do you offer content writing only, or full SEO strategy?
Both. A project can focus on research-backed content production or expand into keyword mapping, on-page optimization, technical review, local SEO, and ongoing recommendations.
Do you offer ongoing SEO support or one-time services?
Both formats are available. One-time work suits audits, research, and defined content batches. Ongoing support suits publishing, optimization, monitoring, and iteration.
What is your typical project timeline and pricing structure?
Timeline and pricing depend on scope, page count, research depth, access, and review cycles. I scope each engagement after a short discovery conversation so deliverables and expectations are clear before work begins.

02 / Process & results

Research, rankings, and ROI

SEO is measured against business goals, not vanity metrics alone.

What is your process for keyword research and content optimization?
I move from business goals and audience questions to keyword discovery, intent analysis, competitor review, opportunity prioritization, content mapping, on-page optimization, and measurement recommendations.
How long does it typically take to see SEO results?
Timing depends on site authority, competition, technical health, publishing consistency, and the goal. Early indexing or engagement changes may appear first, while durable organic growth usually requires sustained work and measurement.
How do you ensure content ranks for competitive keywords?
No ethical SEO provider can guarantee rankings. I improve competitiveness through realistic opportunity analysis, complete search-intent coverage, clear information architecture, credible sourcing, strong on-page fundamentals, and internal linking.
How do you measure success and track ROI?
Measurement is matched to the goal and can include qualified organic traffic, visibility, target-query movement, click-through rate, leads, conversions, and completion of technical improvements. Tracking requirements are agreed before work begins.

03 / Website audits

Findings that lead to action

The goal is a prioritized roadmap, not a stack of unexplained exports.

What deliverables do I receive with a website audit?
A practical audit covers technical health, crawl and index signals, on-page issues, content gaps, and competitor observations. You receive prioritized findings and an action plan organized for implementation.
What tools do you use for audits?
The toolset depends on access and scope. It may include Google Search Console, Google Analytics, Semrush, Google PageSpeed Insights, structured-data validators, and manual SERP and page reviews.
Will I get an action plan?
Yes. Findings are organized by impact, urgency, and implementation effort so you can see what to address first, what needs developer support, and what can be improved through content.
Can you help with technical SEO if I am on a specific platform?
Technical recommendations can be adapted to your platform and the access available. I first identify what can be changed in the CMS, what needs developer support, and what should be prioritized.

04 / Service details

On-page, links, and local search

Focused answers for specific SEO services.

What makes your on-page optimization different?
I start with search intent and reader usefulness, then align titles, descriptions, headings, copy, internal links, and calls to action. Keywords support clarity rather than being inserted mechanically.
How do you ensure backlinks are high quality?
I review opportunities for topical relevance, editorial context, site quality, audience fit, and signs of manipulation. Relevance and trust matter more than raw volume.
How many local citations do you typically build?
Citation volume depends on the business, location, current listings, and relevant directories. I prioritize accurate, useful coverage rather than promising a fixed number of low-value submissions.
Can you help with local SEO for multiple locations?
Yes. Multi-location work can include location-page planning, Google Business Profile recommendations, citation consistency, local keyword mapping, and a framework for avoiding duplicate location content.
